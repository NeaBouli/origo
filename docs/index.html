HOW ORIGO WORKS

Place your seed. Conway takes over. What survives is yours. Every generation your cells earn GHIFR particles — exchangeable for real $IFR tokens. The algorithm is the only judge.

01
PLACE YOUR SEED
Choose a starting pattern from the library — Glider, Pulsar, Acorn. Place it anywhere in the Known World. This is your only moment of direct control.
02
CONWAY DECIDES
Four mathematical rules run automatically. Cells live, die, and reproduce. No human intervention. No randomness. Pure deterministic mathematics — forever.
03
EARN GHIFR
Every generation your cells survive, you earn GHIFR particles. More territory = more earnings. The pool distributes proportionally — zero-sum, always covered.
04
REDEEM TOKENS
Convert GHIFR to $IFR via a signed claim voucher on the Inferno Protocol. No lockup. 45% of all app revenue continuously buys IFR from the open market.
05
ASCEND LAYERS
Produce a stable emergent structure — Glider, Oscillator, Spaceship — that survives 50+ generations. You ascend to Layer 1. The universe expands around you.
06
SHAPE THE WORLD
Discover a novel pattern — one that has never existed before. The community names it. At Layer 2+, you earn the right to define your own survival rule.

TECHNOLOGY

BUILT TO LAST
FOREVER

Open source. Deterministic. Auditable. The mathematics is the contract — no admin can change the rules mid-game.

⚙️
RUST ENGINE
Phase 2 Conway + Lenia core. 1000×1000 grid at 10 ticks/sec on a €6/month server. Zero garbage collection pauses.
🌐
NODE.JS + WEBSOCKET
Delta-only broadcast — only changed cells transmitted. 500 concurrent connections on a single CX21 node. Reconnect = full snapshot.
🎮
THREE.JS + LENIA
3D rotating planet. Instanced cells. WebGL Gaussian shader (Phase 1 Lenia rendering). 3 zoom levels. Drag-rotate. Pinch-zoom. Mobile-ready.
🔐
EIP-712 VOUCHERS
Server signs GHIFR claims with ECDSA. Inferno Protocol verifies on-chain. The game never handles tokens. Nonce registry prevents replay. 24h expiry.
🗄️
POSTGRES + REDIS
Grid state in Redis RAM — zero DB reads per tick. PostgreSQL for double-entry GHIFR ledger, voucher nonce registry, fossil table, pattern votes.
🔥
INFERNO PROTOCOL
$IFR — deflationary ERC-20. 2.5% burned per transfer. 45% of ORIGO revenue continuously buys IFR from Uniswap. GameClaimPool distributes to players.
